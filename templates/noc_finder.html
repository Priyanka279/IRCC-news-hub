What is TEER?

Training, Education, Experience and Responsibilities. TEER 0–3 qualifies for Express Entry. TEER 4–5 does not qualify for FSW/CEC streams.

CRS Points from Work

Canadian work in TEER 0–3: up to 80 pts (3+ yrs). Foreign work in TEER 0–3: up to 40 pts. Work outside TEER 0–3 = 0 pts in Express Entry.

Job Offer Bonus

Valid LMIA-backed job offer in TEER 1/2/3 = +50 CRS pts. Senior manager or C-suite (NOC 00) = +200 CRS pts.

Skilled Trades (TEER 2)

Electricians, plumbers, welders qualify under Federal Skilled Trades (FST) stream. Different eligibility rules than FSW — no post-secondary needed.
