🍁 Ontario — Best For Masters Grads
The OINP Masters Graduate stream requires no job offer, making it ideal for recent graduates. Apply within 2 years of graduation. Very competitive — express interest as soon as the stream opens each year.
🌊 BC — Best For Tech Industry
BC Tech Pilot targets specific NOC codes in software and engineering. No minimum EE score for some roles. Vancouver ecosystem is strong for tech, though cost of living is very high.
⛰ Alberta — Best For Lower Cost
Alberta has no provincial income tax and lower housing costs. AAIP draws for healthcare, agriculture, and skilled trades. Processing times are fast. Growing tech scene in Calgary.
❄️ Quebec — French Required
Quebec has a completely separate immigration system (not Express Entry). You must apply through MIFI and French proficiency (B2+) is required. Not suitable without French skills.
